Courses We Offer

We offer a comprehensive range of courses from Pre-Primary to Secondary. Our curriculum emphasizes a balanced approach, blending core subjects with extracurricular activities to ensure holistic development.

Pre-Primary

Our Pre-Primary program nurtures young minds through play-based learning and interactive activities. We focus on developing social skills, creativity, and a love for learning in a warm and caring environment.

Primary

The Primary curriculum builds a strong academic foundation while fostering curiosity and critical thinking. With a balanced approach to education, students explore a range of subjects, laying the groundwork for lifelong learning.

Secondary

Our Secondary courses challenge students to deepen their knowledge and skills across core subjects. Emphasizing academic excellence, personal growth, and preparation for higher education, we support students in achieving their full potential.

Our Facilities

At New Modern School, we pride ourselves on offering top-notch facilities that support our students' academic and personal growth. Our world-class library provides a vast array of resources for research and reading. The largest playground in the area ensures students have plenty of space for physical activities and sports.

Engaged in Learning

New Modern School offers cutting-edge classrooms with the latest educational technology, ensuring interactive and personalized learning. Our dedicated teachers and small class sizes foster critical thinking and academic excellence, making us a top choice for quality education.

Celebrating Achievement

At New Modern School, we celebrate student achievements in a fully equipped auditorium, encouraging a culture of excellence. Our recognition of success across academics, sports, and the arts motivates students to reach their full potential.

Annual Day Celebration
